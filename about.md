## About This Library

This is a personal investment thesis library built to document and track a portfolio structured around Ray Dalio's All Weather principles from *"Investing in Light of the Big Cycle."*

Every position in the portfolio is backed by a written thesis that explains its purpose, its alignment with the broader investment framework, and the conditions under which it would be rebalanced or exited.

## Investment Philosophy

The portfolio emphasizes **balanced diversification** across the four key economic drivers that Dalio identifies:

- **Growth** — captured through a 55–60% equity allocation spanning broad market, value, quality compounders, and high-conviction names
- **Inflation** — hedged through real assets (gold + broad commodities) at 14–16%
- **Risk premiums** — managed through tactical dry powder (SGOV T-bills) at 14–17%
- **Discount rates** — addressed through geographic and asset-class diversification

The design deliberately avoids U.S.-only survivorship bias and protects purchasing power when financial wealth is devalued relative to tangible assets.

## How It Works

- **Theses** document the rationale for each position or asset class in the portfolio
- **Dashboard** shows real-time portfolio allocations, live market prices, and deployment tools
- **Rebalancing rules** enforce mechanical buy-low/sell-high discipline
