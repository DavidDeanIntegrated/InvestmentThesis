Methodology

This portfolio is structured entirely around Ray Dalio's principles from "Investing in Light of the Big Cycle." It emphasizes balanced diversification across the four key drivers — growth, inflation, risk premiums, and discount rates — to survive late-cycle environments of currency devaluation and financial-asset destruction.

Every position serves a specific purpose within the All Weather framework. The 55–60% equity allocation is growth-tilted for a longer time horizon, while 14–16% in real assets (gold + commodities) and 14–17% in dry powder provide structural protection and tactical optionality.
